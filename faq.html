Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful Information for Visitors

Is admission free?

Yes. Jinniuling Park is open to the public free of charge.


Can visitors feed pigeons?

Yes. Pigeon Square is one of the most popular attractions for families.


Are sports facilities available?

Yes. Visitors can access designated sports and fitness facilities.


Is the Martyrs Memorial Area open to visitors?

Yes. Visitors are welcome to pay respect and learn about local history.


What is the best time to visit?

Early morning and late afternoon are ideal because temperatures are more comfortable.


Can I take photographs?

Yes. Photography is encouraged throughout the park.
