Frequently Asked Questions

Is admission free?

Yes. Public access is free.


Can visitors feed pigeons?

Yes. Pigeon Square is a popular attraction.


Are sports facilities available?

Yes. Visitors can use designated sports areas.


Is the Martyrs Memorial Area open?

Yes. Visitors can pay respects and learn about local history.


What is the best time to visit?

Early morning and late afternoon are ideal due to comfortable temperatures.
