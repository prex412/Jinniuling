Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful Information for Visitors

Is admission free?

Yes. Jinniuling Park is open free of charge.


Can visitors feed pigeons?

Yes. Feeding pigeons is one of the most popular family activities.


Are sports facilities available?

Yes. Multiple sports facilities are available throughout the Sports Zone.


Can I visit the Martyrs Memorial Area?

Yes. Visitors are welcome to learn about local history and pay respects.


What is the best time to visit?

Early morning and late afternoon offer the most comfortable weather.


Can I take photographs?

Photography is encouraged throughout the park.
