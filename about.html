About Jinniuling Park

Three Distinct Zones, One Unique Experience

Park Overview

One of Haikou's most important urban recreational and ecological parks.

Jinniuling Park is one of the largest urban parks in Haikou City. The park combines recreational facilities, ecological landscapes, and cultural attractions. It is an important destination for both local residents and tourists.


The park provides opportunities for sports, leisure, environmental education, family recreation, and patriotic learning.

Park Zones

Sports Zone

Includes football fields, basketball courts, table tennis facilities, skateboarding areas, and outdoor fitness equipment.

Martyrs Memorial Zone

A patriotic education area featuring the Martyrs Memorial Monument and Martyrs Cemetery.

Ecological Park Zone

A green landscape area featuring lakes, tropical vegetation, walking trails, and pigeon square.
