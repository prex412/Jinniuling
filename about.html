About Jinniuling Park

Three Distinct Zones, One Unique Experience

Park Overview


Jinniuling Park is one of Haikou’s most important urban parks, combining recreation, ecology and cultural heritage.

Sports Zone

Football, basketball, table tennis, skateboarding and fitness facilities.

Martyrs Memorial Zone

Patriotic education area with memorial monuments and cemetery.

Ecological Park Zone

Tropical vegetation, scenic lakes and walking trails.
