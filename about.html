About Jinniuling Park

A multifunctional urban park combining recreation, history, culture, and ecology.

Park Overview


Jinniuling Park is one of the largest urban parks in Haikou City. The park offers recreational facilities, ecological landscapes, and cultural attractions that serve both residents and tourists.

Sports Zone

The Sports Zone provides football fields, basketball courts, table tennis facilities, skateboarding areas, and outdoor fitness equipment.

Martyrs Memorial Zone

This area includes the Martyrs Memorial Monument and Cemetery, serving as a patriotic education destination and memorial site.

Ecological Park Zone

Visitors can enjoy tropical vegetation, scenic lakes, pigeon squares, open lawns, and relaxing walking trails.
