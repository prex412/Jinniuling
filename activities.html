Activities in Jinniuling Park

Enjoy recreation, sports, leisure, and cultural experiences.

Skateboarding

The skateboarding area is popular among young visitors and sports enthusiasts.

Football

Visitors can enjoy football matches and outdoor training activities.

Basketball

Public basketball courts provide opportunities for recreation and exercise.

Table Tennis

Table tennis facilities attract local residents of all ages.

Family Camping

Large open lawns create ideal spaces for family picnics and camping activities.

Feeding Pigeons

The pigeon square is one of the most popular family attractions in the park.

Memorial Visits

Visitors can pay respect to national heroes at the Martyrs Memorial Area.

Walking & Jogging

Shaded trails provide a relaxing environment for walking and exercise.
