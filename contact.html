Official Information of Haikou Jinniuling Park Management Office

Haikou Jinniuling Park Management Office


The Haikou Jinniuling Park Management Office (Haikou Institute of Landscape Science) is responsible for implementing urban park management regulations, maintaining park landscapes and facilities, protecting biodiversity, conducting plant conservation and scientific research, managing public security and sanitation, and promoting horticultural innovation and environmental education.


Management Responsibilities

The Management Office is responsible for:

  • Urban park management and policy implementation
  • Landscape maintenance and facility management
  • Biodiversity conservation and plant protection
  • Public safety and sanitation management
  • Pest monitoring and control
  • Scientific research in landscape and horticulture
  • Flower production and cultivation
  • Introduction, domestication, and promotion of new flower varieties
  • Development and promotion of horticultural technologies

Departments

  • Office Administration
  • Comprehensive Affairs Department
  • Security Department
  • Research & Production Department
  • Landscape Management Department
